Get Into It

Learn to care for your crown in the way that it needs.

Step 1: Detangling

Before cleansing your hair for a new protective style, you must gently detangle beforehand to minimize breakage. Separate your hair into four equal parts. Apply leave-in conditioner as well as an oil to the first section. Using a wide tooth comb, gently comb from the end to root until the comb slides effortlessly with each pass. Repeat for every section until the entire head is fully detangled.

Step 2: Cleanse

Apply non-sulfate shampoo to the roots of your hair and lather in a circular motion. No need to saturate your entire shaft; the shampoo will slide down to cleanse the rest of your hair. Rinse thoroughly and repeat two more times.

Step 3: Deep Condition

After rinsing the shampoo from your hair, section your hair off into four parts once more, and apply deep conditioner to each section. Secure the heat with a plastic cap and let it sit for at least one hour.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Rinse the conditioner and follow up with your favorite leave-in conditioner, as well as a dime-sized amount of oil to seal it in. Do this in the four sections from earlier. Blow dry on medium heat setting.

Step 5: Styling

You can choose to rock the fro or put your hair in braids/twists until it's time for your protective style! Remember not to apply any products in the meantime.
